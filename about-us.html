About VIPRESA Care

Vipresa Care is a leading care provider with over 15 years experience. We provide highly trained carers to organizations such as Nursing Homes, Hospitals, Domiciliary Care Agencies, Assisted Living Homes, and Care Homes. Our carers are reliable, professional, and knowledgeable in all aspects of care. 

With their extensive knowledge and expertise, our carers are able to offer a personalised level of support to all their clients according to their needs and preferences. Our aim is to support clients to maintain their independence whilst having their choices and wishes regarding their care adhered to as much as legally possible. 

Whatever your condition or your needs, Vipresa Care will assist you to improve your quality of life. 

How It Works 

We have taken every precaution to make hiring a carer as secure, easy, and stress-free as we can since we recognize that it is an emotionally charged decision. We've made it possible to accommodate your request for a carer at short notice. We provide highly trained carers to support individuals in developing independent living skills that will enable them to look after themselves. We provide support with particular aspects of day-to-day life such as managing money, staying healthy, cooking, taking part in the community, or going to college.

We can organize activities such as shopping trips, and visits to the theatre or cinema or we can simply be with individuals in their own homes to help them cope with day-to-day living
Vipresa Care is built around choices, and we empower people to take charge of their lives by establishing direct connections between carers and those who need care.
We are creating a caring community where each of us can live a life of independence.
 

Why You Should Choose Vipresa Care? 

 We provide a wide range of care services for individuals. Our services are tailored to each client, based on their individual needs and are delivered by the best carers in the industry.

Our clients receive the entire amount of time that they pay for and are never rushed.

Before they begin receiving care, carers are carefully matched and, if possible, introduced before delivering care.

We are always fast to respond, diligent in our duty of care, and always strive to make our clients smile.

Our Carers 

When it comes to choosing a Carer for yourself or a loved one, you'll want to ensure that you're getting the best care available. We only employ the best carers who are dedicated to providing exceptional care and support; the kind of care we would want for our own family.

We only choose Carers who have a genuine passion for helping others. Part of this motivation is to ensure that they are always doing everything possible to improve the quality of life for those in their care as well as their families.

Trusted and Professional Carers 

 At Vipresa Care, our Carers undergo high-quality training and are strongly encouraged to complete ongoing training and external credentials to support their continued personal and professional growth.

Our primary goal is always the safety and well-being of the people in our care. All our employees are subjected to enhanced criminal background checks and must submit at least two references, one of which must be from their previous workplace. Employees are also subjected to identity checks to confirm that they are legally permitted to work in the United Kingdom.
