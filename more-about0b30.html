Trusted and Professional Carers

At Comfort Cove Home Care, our Carers undergo high-quality training and are strongly encouraged to complete ongoing training and external credentials to support their continued personal and professional growth. Our primary goal is always the safety and well-being of the people in our care. All our employees are subjected to enhanced criminal background checks and must submit at least two references, one of which must be from their previous workplace. Employees are also subjected to identity checks to confirm that they are legally permitted to work in the United Kingdom.
