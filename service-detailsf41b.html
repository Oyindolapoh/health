Dementia Care

Living with dementia or caring for someone with dementia can be challenging and often heart breaking. Having to adjust to a world that is unfamiliar and struggling to cope with deteriorating cognitive function such as memory, problem solving, thinking and perception.  

We are here to help, At Vipresa Care we have experienced and trained carers that are able to offer full memory care support whether it be in offering emotional support, respite break, having the same carer whenever needed; for familiarity and consistency. We will do all that we can to make the any adaption or transition as simple as possible, whilst managing your changing needs with respect, understanding and dignity
