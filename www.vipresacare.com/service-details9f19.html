Elder care

At Vipresa Care, we aim to improve the quality of life for the elderly, We can assist you if you or a loved one requires some extra support and assistance. We are here to assistance you any time of the day to 24-hour support, which is tailored to your specific needs, all from the comfort of your own home.

Our homecare services can assist elders with daily living tasks and activities that have become challenging to manage or carry out. As we age, we struggle with certain aspects that we were able to perform effortlessly before. Therefore, we provide a range of care services with you at the forefront to make things as simple as possible with your input and choices.

Our dedicated and professional carers will assist with personal care, shopping, meal preparation/cooking, household chores, accompanying to appointments and companionship.
